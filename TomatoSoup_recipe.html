tomato soup recipé

Fresh tomatoes are the stars of this classic summertime tomato soup recipé. Low-fat milk and light cream cheese keep it healthy.

Duration:

20 mins

Yield:

4 (serving size: 1 1/4 cups)
extra-virgin olive oil 1 tablespoon
prechopped onion 1½ cups
garlic cloves, chopped 3
chopped fresh basil ¾ cup
can fire-roasted diced tomatoes, undrained 1 (28-ounce)
1/3-less-fat cream cheese, cut into cubes ½ cup (4 ounces)
1% low-fat milk 2 cups
salt ¼ teaspoon
black pepper ¼ teaspoon
Cooking spray
garlic clove, halved 1
shredded Asiago cheese 1 ounce
  1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add onion; sauté 3 minutes.
  3. Stir in garlic; cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add basil and tomatoes
  5. Bring to a boil.
  6. Stir in cheese until melted.
  7. Place mixture in blender, and blend until smooth.
  8. Return to pan; stir in milk, salt, and pepper.
  9. Cook 2 minutes.
